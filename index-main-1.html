What is Traditional Chinese Medicine

Chinese traditional herbal tea is base on Traditional Chinese medicine (TCM). TCM has been around for over a thousand years,duringthe late Qing dynasty medicine scholars believed that natural plantscan solve diseases through the balance of Qi. A man named ShengLong, tried thousands natural plants and recorded every plants relative function to the human body, this record forms thecomprehensive codex of Chinese herbal medicine. After thousands of years of evolution, there are hundred recipe of herbal medicine,each recipe has their specific function to human body.
