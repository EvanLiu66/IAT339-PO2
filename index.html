What is herbal tea

Chinese traditional herbal tea is base on Traditional Chinese medicine (TCM). TCM has been around for over a thousand years,during the late Qing dynasty medicine scholars believed that natural plants can solve diseases through the balance of Qi. A man named Sheng Long, tried thousands natural plants and recorded every plants relative function to the human body, this record forms the comprehensive codex of Chinese herbal medicine. After thousands of years of evolution, there are hundred recipe of herbal medicine, each recipe has their specific function to human body.
